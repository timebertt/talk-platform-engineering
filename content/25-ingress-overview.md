## What Does This Teach Us?

- Today, it might be `Ingress`
- Tomorrow, it might be `Gateway`
- The landscape is constantly evolving
- We need to design our platform to be adaptable and flexible
- Avoid tight coupling to specific technologies
- Not only for this lecture: learning the concepts is more important than knowing the implementations
